How to use VR:

Using a virtual reality headset is straightforward. First, connect it to a compatible device, such as a PC or gaming console. Put on the headset and adjust the straps for a snug fit. Navigate the menu using controllers or hand gestures to select VR experiences. Enjoy immersive 3D environments and interactions.
